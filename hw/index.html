Relay Controller

  • Supply voltage: 12V DC
  • Number of Relays: 4
  • Number of I/O Channels: 6
  • Relay Rating: 10A / 110V AC or 24V DC
  • I/O channels configurable as digital input/output, analog input or temperature sensor
  • A/D Converter Resolution: 10-bit
  • Compatible with Maxim/Dallas DS18B20 1-Wire Temperature Sensors
  • Dimensions: 4.11" x 2.14"

DC Power Supplies

  • Supply voltage: 9 to 15V DC or 6 to 12V AC adapter
  • Variable Mode: adjustable from 1.2 to 12V DC
  • Fixed Mode: 3.3V or 5V DC
  • Maximum output current: 300 mA
  • Short-circuit protection
  • Dimensions: 2.17" x 0.99"

Audio Amplifiers

  • Supply voltage: 8 to 16V DC / 0.5 to 1 A
  • Based around two TDA2003 Integrated Circuit
  • Music Power: > 5W @ 4 Ohm
  • RMS Power: 3.5W @ 4 Ohm / 2W @ 8 Ohm
  • Frequency response: 40 Hz to 15 KHz
  • Includes volume controls and 3.5mm stereo input jack

LED Blinkers

  • Supply voltage: 5 to 14V DC / 200mA
  • Input sensitivity: adjustable from approx. 75mV
  • Based on two AN6884 Integrated Circuits
  • Dimensions: 1.42" x 2.37"

Color Organs

  • Supply voltage: 6 to 24V DC / 150 mA
  • 8 channels / 20 programs
  • PIC Microcontroller with speed adjustment control
  • Opto-isolated outputs
  • Maximum current per channel: 1A Max.
  • Dimensions: 4.80" x 4.79"

Motor Controllers

  • Supply / load voltage: 6 to 16V DC
  • Maximum continuous current: 5A @ 140 Hz
  • Duty Cycle adjustable from 1 to 99%
  • Frequency: approximately 140Hz
  • Includes heatsink
  • Dimensions: 1.77" x 1.18"

Thermometers

  • Supply voltage: regulated 5V DC / 150mA
  • DS18B20 temperature sensor
  • 4-Digit 0.56" 7-segment display
  • Degree Celsius or Degree Fahrenheit
  • Temperature Range: -55°C to 125°C
  • Resolution: ±0.5°C

Signal Generators

  • Supply voltage: 12V AC adapter or transformer
  • Frequency range: approx. 10Hz to 200KHz
  • Two RCA jack outputs: sine/triangle and square/pulse
  • Amplitude and frequency controls for sine and triangle waveforms
  • Square/Pulse maximum output amplitude: 12Vpp fixed
  • Low sine wave distortion, typically 0.5%
